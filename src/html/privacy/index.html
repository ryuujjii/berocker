Privacy Policy

At BeRocker, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SaaS CRM services (“Services”). By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide when registering for an account or using our Services. This may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, billing details, and any other information necessary to deliver our services.

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your interactions with our Services, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and the time spent on our platform.

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage patterns, and deliver personalized content. You can adjust your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and Maintaining Services: To create and manage your account, process transactions, and deliver customer support.
  • Improving Our Platform: To analyze usage trends, enhance functionality, and optimize user experience.
  • Communications: To send updates, important notifications, and marketing materials if you have opted in.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and to protect our legal rights.

3. Sharing Your Information

We value your privacy and do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating our platform and delivering services, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process to disclose your information.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, where your data may be part of the transferred assets, subject to confidentiality protections.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While we strive to secure your data, no method of transmission over the Internet or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When your data is no longer required, we will securely dispose of it.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access and Correction: Request access to and correction of your personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and contractual restrictions.
  • Opt-Out: Withdraw your consent or opt out of receiving marketing communications.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a commonly used format.
