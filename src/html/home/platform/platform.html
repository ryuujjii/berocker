#whatIs

What is BeRocker?

BeRocker is an AI-powered CRM designed for auto transport brokers, featuring all the software, integrations, and resources you need for efficient operations. Our connected platform helps you grow your business faster by focusing on what matters most: your customers.

Get a demo to explore our PRO software, or start with our essential tools and upgrade as you grow.

#omnichannel

Never lose a client
again

How many potential clients and inquiries are you missing? BeRocker CRM automatically captures all leads and customer requests from every channel and funnels them into your sales pipeline. Whether it’s web forms, emails, calls, social media, or chats – never lose a potential customer again.

#call

One-click
Calling

BeRocker integrates with over five leading US telephony providers, enabling one-click outbound calls and seamless inbound call handling directly from client profiles. Every call—along with its history, recordings, and performance stats—is automatically saved, so you never miss an incoming call.

Get comprehensive call analytics to monitor your team's performance, while BeRocker AI Call ensures no incoming call goes unanswered.
